Tape your bff to a wall

Description

This pretty harmless recipe for disaster can be enjoyed at home with a couple of friends and drinks.

Ingredients

  • 1 wall
  • 1 chair
  • 1 duct tape
  • 1 accomplice
  • 1 willing victim
  • 1 phone with camera

Steps

  1. Place the chair against the wall.
  2. Ask your willing victim to stand over the chair with their back against the wall.
  3. Start recording with your phone's camera.
  4. Proceed to tape your willing victim to the wall with the help of your accomplice.
  5. Once the willing victim seems to be firmly taped, remove the chair from under their feet.
  6. Observe the willing victim's face as they stay suspended against the wall amused and totally ignorant about your true intentions
  7. You can now dance and celebrate your treachery.
  8. Do not stop the recording and wait for the fall.
  9. Upload your video to the Internet for extra laughs.
